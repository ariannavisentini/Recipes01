CARBONARA

Ingredients

  • Large eggs
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Bacon
  • Spaghetti
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Meanwhile, place the eggs, cheese, and salt in a medium bowl and whisk to combine
  2. Add the spaghetti to the water and cook. Meanwhile, cook the bacon until crisp.
  3. Drain the pasta and add to the pot with the bacon grease.
  4. While whisking the egg mixture, while tossing the pasta constantly, slowly pour in the egg mixture.
  5. Transfer pasta to bowls, sprinkle with the reserved bacon, more cheese and serve very hot.

    3.  
  3. Cook until most of the liquid has been absorbed. Add another cup of broth and keep cooking.
    4.  
  4. When the rice is ready, stir in the cheese. Add a little more broth to give the risotto the consistency you like Season with salt and pepper.

NUTELLA CREPES

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • 00 flour
  • Butter
  • Nutella
  • Icing sugar

Preparation

  1. Beat the eggs with a fork and add the milk. Mix the ingredients well with a fork.
  2. In a frying pan greased with a knob of butter. Pour a ladleful of the mixture to cover almost the entire surface of the pan.
  3. Turn it on the other side until it is golden brown, take a spoonful of Nutella and spread it on the surface of the crepe.
  4. Fold the crepe and sprinkle with icing sugar and serve your crepe warm.
  5. Add toppings or fruits to taste.

MUFFINS

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Vanilia
  • Flour
  • Butter

Preparation

  1. Melt the butter and leave to cool. In the meantime, combine all the liquid ingredients in a jug.
  2. Then add sugar, a pinch of salt.
  3. Place the whisk of an electric mixer in the bowl with the powders and turn it on very low speed while adding the liquid mixture a little at a time.
  4. Pour about 90 grams of the mixture into 12 ramekins.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven, in static mode, at 180° for 35 minutes. Enjoy!

COCONUT PRALINES

Ingredients

  • Coconut
  • Icing Sugar
  • Ricotta

Preparation

  1. Start by sifting the ricotta into a bowl to make it creamier, then add the icing sugar and mix everything together.
  2. Add coconut and mix again until it is evenly incorporated.
  3. Transfer it to the palm of your hand and work it into a ball.
  4. Put them in the refrigerator for about 10 minutes before serving so that they compact.
  5. Eat and enjoy!
